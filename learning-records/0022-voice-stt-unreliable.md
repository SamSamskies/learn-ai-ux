# Voice STT feels unreliable in practice

Learner feedback after lesson 0019: Realtime voice transcription often mishears them; voice mode feels frustrating for precise intent. Treat this as a product constraint, not a personal setup quirk.

**Implications:** Prefer screen confirmation/edit for any tool whose args come from spoken intent (especially image prompts). Don't teach "just trust the transcript." Keep handoff-to-text as the escape hatch when precision matters.
